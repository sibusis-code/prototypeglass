Anti-Smash & Grab

Protect yourself, your passengers, and your valuables

What Is Anti-Smash & Grab Film?

Anti-Smash & Grab security film is a transparent polyester film applied to the interior surface of your vehicle's windows. It holds shattered glass together upon impact, making it significantly harder for criminals to break through and grab your belongings — or harm you.

South Africa has one of the highest smash-and-grab crime rates in the world. Installing safety film is one of the most affordable and effective ways to protect yourself at traffic lights, parking lots, and wherever you drive.

SABS Approved Installation

All our films meet South African Bureau of Standards requirements and come with a certificate of fitment.

Why Install Anti-Smash & Grab?

Multiple layers of protection for you and your vehicle

Personal Safety

Prevents glass shards from injuring you and your passengers during an attempted break-in or accident.

Protect Valuables

Criminals can't simply punch through and grab your phone, laptop, handbag, or other items.

UV Protection

Blocks up to 99% of harmful UV rays, protecting your skin and preventing interior fading.

Heat Reduction

Reduces interior heat by up to 60%, keeping your car cooler and reducing AC load.

Privacy & Security

Tinted options reduce visibility into your vehicle, deterring opportunistic criminals.

Insurance Savings

Many insurers offer reduced premiums when anti-smash film is installed with a valid certificate.
