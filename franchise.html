Become a Franchisee

Build Your Future With Prototype Glass

Prototype Glass Windscreen is expanding across South Africa and we're looking for driven entrepreneurs to join our growing network. With over 25 years in the auto glass industry, we've perfected a business model that delivers consistent results.

As a franchisee, you'll benefit from our established brand reputation, SABS-approved supplier relationships, comprehensive training programme, and ongoing operational support — everything you need to hit the ground running.

The auto glass industry in South Africa is a multi-billion rand market with steady demand. Vehicles need glass — whether it's accident damage, stone chips, or wear and tear. This means consistent, year-round revenue for franchise owners.
