Make extra cash by selling your auto-glass to us

Turn Your Auto Glass Into Cash

Do you have new, unused, or surplus auto glass lying around? Whether you're a workshop closing down, a parts supplier with overstock, or a car owner with leftover glass from an insurance claim — we want to buy it from you.

Prototype Glass purchases quality auto glass at competitive prices. We accept windscreens, rear screens, door glass, quarter glass, and more for a wide range of vehicle makes and models.

Fast Payment

We evaluate your glass on the spot and pay via EFT or cash within 24 hours of acceptance.

What We Buy

We accept a wide range of auto glass types and brands

Windscreens

New or unused windscreens for all major vehicle brands — Toyota, VW, BMW, Ford, Mercedes, and more.

Rear Screens

Heated and non-heated rear screens for sedans, hatchbacks, SUVs, and bakkies.

Door Glass

Front and rear door glass for all vehicle types, including tempered and laminated options.

Quarter Glass
