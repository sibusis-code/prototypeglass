Know When

Repair vs. Replacement

Not every chip needs a full windscreen replacement. Here's how to know which service you need.

Repair

  • Chip smaller than a R5 coin
  • Crack shorter than 15cm
  • Damage not in driver's direct line of sight
  • Crack hasn't reached the edge
  • Only 1-2 chips on the windscreen

VS

Replace

  • Crack longer than 15cm
  • Multiple cracks or branching
  • Damage in driver's line of sight
  • Crack reaches the screen edge
  • 3+ chips on the windscreen

Simple Process

How Windscreen Repair Works

Our repair process is quick, proven, and hassle-free

1. Inspect

We assess the damage to determine if a repair is possible or if replacement is needed.

2. Inject Resin

SABS-approved resin is injected under pressure into the chip or crack to fill and seal it.

3. UV Cure

The resin is cured with UV light, hardening it to restore full structural strength.

4. Polish & Done

We polish the surface smooth. The repair is nearly invisible and your windscreen is roadworthy.
