Our Story

25+ Years of Excellence in Auto Glass

Prototype Glass Windscreen has been serving the Randburg community and greater Johannesburg since 1995. Located at 373 Jan Smuts Ave in Craighall Park, we've built our reputation on quality workmanship, honest pricing, and exceptional customer service.

We specialize in professional auto glass replacement and repair for all vehicle makes and models — from everyday commuters to luxury and commercial vehicles. Our SABS-approved glass and certified technicians ensure every installation meets the highest safety standards.

Whether you need a windscreen replacement, chip repair, or help with an insurance claim, our team is here to make the process seamless.
